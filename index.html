How It Works.

Get In Touch

Use one of the contact methods to get in touch and we will help you with your recycling situation.

In the case of confidential waste, we will make a pre-collection visit to provide you with secure tear-resistant confidential sacks that you can fill and store safely ready for collection.

Or, if you are just enquiring or looking for advice on your waste situation, get in touch and we can help.

Collect

Once a collection has been arranged you can expect a regular and reliable collection from our vetted, friendly staff who can ring en route if desired.

The waste will be swiftly transported and loaded onto the alarmed vehicle by our staff respecting your work environment.

Once loaded, your waste is under our care and is then monitored until destruction. We will provide a transfer note as proof of collection and will take the waste away for processing.

Process

Sensitive, confidential material will be taken promptly to a BS-EN 16713 document destruction facility, to ISO 9001:2008 quality standards.

Material is then shredded and destroyed in an enclosed, controlled environment, monitored by CCTV and alarm systems by a team of professionals.

Non-sentive material such as cardboard is taken to a recycling facility where 100% of the material is recycled for repurposing.

FAQ

My business handles sensitive data, how secure is the destruction process?

We understand the importance of keeping information secure. We ensure that any material due to be shredded is transported under our personal supervision and destroyed in a secure facility the same day.

We transport confidential material in strong, tear-resistant sacks with security ties. This ensures the material is contained safely and will remain confidential right through the destruction process.

We will personally notify you by email or phone-call when your material is delivered to the facility and you receive a certificate of destruction once destruction is complete and processed.

We have a lot of information that needs destroying, is the process expensive?

We charge a lot less than the larger destruction companies because we destroy the material off-site and due to our being a smaller, family run business.

This process is still secure and still prompt. Your material is delivered and destroyed to the facility the same day but your company pays a lot less by avoiding extra charges such as transporting of the material, time on site and other unforseen charges.

You are simply charged a price per bag collected. This charge is negotiated with the price varying based on the amount of material and regularity of collections. We are highly competitive on our pricing.

How often will my waste be collected?

We recommend a monthly collection.

For most companies this is enough time to accumulate enough material ready for destruction avoiding keeping confidential material longer than necessary.

Some companies and individuals however don't require so regular a collection, or even require a one-off service. This is no problem and we can arrange this for you.

What areas do you cover?

Our main service area is central Birmingham and the outlying areas.



We are happy to help arrange collections from a variety of locations, please get in touch and to discuss.
